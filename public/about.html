About Me

My name is Ashton Greyvenstein, and I am a junior developer with a passion for learning and growing in the tech world. I enjoy solving problems, exploring new ideas, and improving my skills as I work toward building a successful future. Even though I am still early in my career, I am determined to work hard and make a name for myself in the industry.

Baseball is especially important to me because it gives me a sense of teamwork, competition, and motivation to improve both on and off the field.

People would describe me as funny and a little weird in the best way possible. I enjoy making others laugh and being myself without worrying too much about fitting in. My biggest goals are to become successful in my work as a developer or possibly even in baseball one day. No matter which path I follow, I want to build a future that I can be proud of.
